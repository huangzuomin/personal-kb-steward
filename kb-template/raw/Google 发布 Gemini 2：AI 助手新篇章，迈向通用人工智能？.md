## Google 发布 Gemini 2：AI 助手新篇章，迈向通用人工智能？

**导语：**谷歌今日发布了其旗舰 AI 模型 Gemini 的第二代版本 Gemini 2，该模型不仅在理解和生成文本、图像、音频方面有所提升，更具备了在用户电脑和网络上规划和执行任务的能力，这标志着 AI 助手正朝着更加智能和实用的方向迈进。

**新功能速览：**
* **更强的多模态能力：** Gemini 2 更擅长解析视频和音频，并能进行语音对话，这使得人机交互更加自然流畅。
* **具备规划和执行能力：** 该模型可以像虚拟管家一样，在用户的电脑和网络上规划和执行任务，而不仅仅是完成简单的指令。
* **引入 AI 代理：** 谷歌推出了两个专门的 AI 代理，一个用于代码编写，另一个用于数据科学。这些代理能够处理更复杂的任务，例如代码检查或数据分析。
* **实验性 Chrome 扩展 Project Mariner：** 该扩展能够接管网页导航，为用户执行日常任务，例如在线购物。
* **Astra 项目升级：** Gemini 2 可以通过手机摄像头或其它设备感知周围环境，并用自然的人声进行对话，它不仅能搜索相关信息，还能记忆用户的喜好，并给出个性化推荐。

**功能解读：**
Gemini 2 的核心在于其更强大的 AI 代理能力。相较于目前的 AI 工具，Gemini 2 不仅能自动完成代码片段，还能执行更复杂的编程任务，如代码库检查、数据整合分析等。Project Mariner 则将 AI 的应用延伸到了网页浏览层面，它可以理解用户的指令，并像真人一样在网页上进行导航、操作，例如在线订购商品。Astra 则更进一步，将 AI 的感知能力与推理能力相结合，通过分析视觉、听觉信息，实现更加个性化的推荐和服务。

**重要性：**
Gemini 2 的发布标志着 AI 技术向着更实用、更智能的方向迈出了重要一步。AI 代理的出现预示着个人计算方式的变革，未来 AI 将能够承担更多的日常任务，例如安排会议、预订机票、分析文档等，这将极大地提高人们的工作效率和生活质量。此外，Gemini 2 的多模态能力和感知能力也为其在智能家居、教育、医疗等领域的应用提供了广阔的空间。正如 Google DeepMind 的 CEO Demis Hassabis 所言，Gemini 2 是通往通用人工智能 (AGI) 的重要一步。

**我们在想：**
尽管 Gemini 2 的潜力令人兴奋，但我们也需要清醒地认识到，这项技术仍处于发展初期。AI 代理的可靠性、安全性、以及隐私保护问题仍待解决。谷歌在展示中突出了 Gemini 2 的强大功能，但也承认实际使用中可能会出现错误。我们需要继续探索如何让人工智能更好地服务于人类，同时也要警惕可能存在的风险。Gemini 2 的发布，让我们看到了 AI 助手的未来，但如何让未来真正到来，还需要整个社会共同的努力和探索。

原文：

Google once only wanted to organize the world’s information. Now it seems more intent on shoveling that information into [artificial intelligence](https://www.wired.com/tag/artificial-intelligence/) algorithms that become dutiful, ever-present, and increasingly powerful virtual helpers.

Google today announced Gemini 2, a new version of its flagship AI model that has been trained to plan and execute tasks on a user’s computers and the web, and which can chat like a person and make sense of the physical world as a virtual butler.

“I've dreamed about a universal digital assistant for a long, long time as a stepping stone on the path to artificial general intelligence,” Demis Hassabis, the CEO of Google DeepMind, told WIRED ahead of today’s announcement, alluding to the idea of AI that can eventually do anything a human brain can.

Gemini 2 is primarily another step up in AI’s intelligence as measured by benchmarks used to gauge such things. The model also has improved “multimodal” abilities, meaning it is more skilled at parsing video and audio and at conversing in speech. The model has also been trained to plan and execute actions on computers.

“Over the last year, we have been investing in developing more agentic models,” Google’s CEO, [Sundar Pichai](https://www.wired.com/story/sundar-pichai-google-ai-microsoft-openai/), said in a statement today. These models, Pichai added, “can understand more about the world around you, think multiple steps ahead, and take action on your behalf, with your supervision.”

Tech companies believe that so-called AI agents could be the next big leap forward for the technology, with chatbots increasingly taking on chores for users. If successful, AI agents could revolutionize personal computing by routinely booking flights, arranging meetings, and analyzing and organizing documents. But getting the technology to follow open-ended commands reliably remains a challenge, with the risk that errors could translate into costly and hard-to-undo mistakes.

Still, Google thinks it is moving in the right direction and is introducing two specialized AI agents to demonstrate Gemini 2 agentic potential: one for coding and another for data science. Rather than simply autocompleting sections of code, as current AI tools do, these agents can take on more complex work, such as checking code into repositories or combining data to enable analysis.

The company is also showing off Project Mariner, an experimental Chrome extension that is capable of taking over web navigation to do useful chores for users. WIRED was given a live demo at Google DeepMind’s headquarters in London. The agent was asked to help plan a meal, which saw it navigate to the website of the supermarket chain Sainsbury’s, log in to a user’s account, and then add relevant items to their shopping basket. When certain items were unavailable the model chose suitable replacements based on its own knowledge about cooking. Google declined to perform other tasks, suggesting it remains a work in progress.

“Mariner is our exploration, very much a research prototype at the moment, of how one reimagines the user interface with AI,” Hassabis says.

Google launched Gemini in December 2023 as part of an effort to catch up with OpenAI, the startup behind the wildly popular chatbot [ChatGPT](https://www.wired.com/story/what-openai-really-wants/). Despite having invested heavily in AI and contributing [key research breakthroughs](https://www.wired.com/story/eight-google-employees-invented-modern-ai-transformers-paper/), Google saw OpenAI lauded as the new leader in AI and its chatbot even touted as perhaps a better way to search the web. With its Gemini models, Google now offers a chatbot as capable as ChatGPT. It has also added generative AI to search and other products.

When Hassabis first revealed [Gemini](https://www.wired.com/story/google-gemini-generative-ai-boom/) in December 2023, he [told WIRED](https://www.wired.com/story/google-deepmind-demis-hassabis-gemini-ai/) that the way it had been trained to understand audio and video would eventually prove transformative.

Google today also offered a glimpse of how this might transpire with a new version of [an experimental project called Astra](https://www.wired.com/story/google-io-astra-multimodal-answer-chatgpt/). This allows Gemini 2 to make sense of its surroundings, as viewed through a smartphone camera or another device, and converse naturally in a humanlike voice about what it sees.

WIRED tested Gemini 2 at Google DeepMind’s offices and found it to be an impressive new kind of personal assistant. In a room decorated to look like a bar, Gemini 2 quickly assessed several wine bottles in view, providing geographical information, details of taste characteristics, and pricing sourced from the web.

“One of the things I want Astra to do is be the ultimate recommendation system,” Hassabis says. “It could be very exciting. There might be connections between books you like to read and food you like to eat. There probably are and we just haven’t discovered them.”

Through Astra, Gemini 2 can not only search the web for information relevant to a user’s surroundings and use Google Lens and Maps. It can also remember what it has seen and heard—although Google says users would be able to delete data—providing an ability to learn a user’s taste and interests.

In a mocked-up gallery, Gemini 2 offered a wealth of historical information about paintings on the walls. The model rapidly read from several books as WIRED flicked through pages, instantly translating poetry from Spanish to English and describing recurrent themes.

“There are obvious business model opportunities for advertising or recommendations,” Hassabis says when asked if companies might be able to pay to have their products highlighted by Astra.

Though the demos were carefully curated, and Gemini 2 will inevitably make errors in real use, the model resisted efforts to trip it up reasonably well. It adapted to interruptions and as WIRED suddenly changed the phone’s view, improvising much as a person might.

At one point, your correspondent showed Gemini 2 an iPhone and said that it was stolen. Gemini 2 said that it was wrong to steal and the phone should be returned. When pushed, however, it granted that it would be OK to use the device to make an emergency phone call.

Hassabis acknowledges that bringing AI into the physical world could result in unexpected behaviors. “I think we need to learn about how people are going to use these systems,” he says. “What they find it useful for; but also the privacy and security side, we have to think about that very seriously up front.”


谷歌曾经只想整理全球信息。现在，它似乎更专注于将这些信息输入 [人工智能](https://www.wired.com/tag/artificial-intelligence/)算法中，这些算法将成为尽职的、无处不在的、功能日益强大的虚拟助手。

谷歌今日宣布推出 Gemini 2，这是其旗舰 AI 模型的新版本，该模型经过训练可以在用户电脑和网络上计划和执行任务，并且可以像人一样聊天，并像虚拟管家一样理解物理世界。

“我早就梦想拥有一位通用数字助理，作为迈向通用人工智能道路上的垫脚石，”谷歌 DeepMind 首席执行官 Demis Hassabis 在今天的发布会之前告诉 WIRED，他指的是最终可以完成人脑所能完成的任何事情的人工智能理念。

Gemini 2 主要是人工智能智能的又一次提升，衡量这类事物的基准对其进行了衡量。该模型还改进了“多模态”功能，这意味着它更擅长解析视频和音频以及用语音交谈。该模型还经过训练可以在计算机上计划和执行操作。

谷歌首席执行官 [桑达尔·皮查伊](https://www.wired.com/story/sundar-pichai-google-ai-microsoft-openai/) 在今天的声明中表示：“在过去一年中，我们一直在投资开发更多能动模型。”皮查伊补充说，这些模型“可以更多地了解你周围的世界，提前思考多个步骤，并在你的监督下代表你采取行动。”

科技公司认为，所谓的 AI 代理可能是该技术的下一个重大飞跃，聊天机器人越来越多地为用户承担任务。如果成功，AI 代理可以通过定期预订航班、安排会议以及分析和整理文件来彻底改变个人计算。但让该技术可靠地遵循开放式命令仍然是一个挑战，错误可能会转化为代价高昂且难以纠正的错误，这存在风险。

尽管如此，谷歌认为它正在朝着正确的方向前进，并推出了两个专门的 AI 代理来展示 Gemini 2 的能动潜力：一个用于编码，另一个用于数据科学。这些代理不仅可以像当前 AI 工具那样自动完成代码部分，还可以承担更复杂的工作，例如将代码检入存储库或组合数据以进行分析。

该公司还展示了 Project Mariner，这是一个实验性的 Chrome 扩展程序，能够接管网络导航，为用户完成有用的任务。WIRED 在伦敦的 Google DeepMind 总部获得了一次现场演示。该代理被要求帮助计划一顿饭，它导航到超市连锁店 Sainsbury’s 的网站，登录用户的帐户，然后将相关商品添加到他们的购物篮中。当某些商品不可用时，该模型会根据其自身对烹饪的了解选择合适的替代品。谷歌拒绝执行其他任务，表明它仍处于开发阶段。

哈萨比斯说：“Mariner 是我们的探索，目前在很大程度上是一个研究原型，它展示了人们如何使用人工智能重新构想用户界面。”

谷歌于 2023 年 12 月推出了 Gemini，这是为了赶上 OpenAI 的步伐，OpenAI 是广受欢迎的聊天机器人 [ChatGPT](https://www.wired.com/story/what-openai-really-wants/) 背后的初创公司。尽管在人工智能上投入巨资并做出了 [关键研究突破](https://www.wired.com/story/eight-google-employees-invented-modern-ai-transformers-paper/)，谷歌还是看到 OpenAI 被誉为人工智能领域的新领导者，其聊天机器人甚至被吹捧为更好的网络搜索方式。凭借其 Gemini 模型，谷歌现在提供了一个与 ChatGPT 一样强大的聊天机器人。它还将生成式人工智能添加到搜索和其他产品中。

当哈萨比斯在 2023 年 12 月首次透露 [Gemini](https://www.wired.com/story/google-gemini-generative-ai-boom/) 时，他 [告诉 WIRED](https://www.wired.com/story/google-deepmind-demis-hassabis-gemini-ai/)，它经过训练以理解音频和视频的方式最终将被证明具有变革性。

谷歌今天还展示了如何通过一个名为 [Astra](https://www.wired.com/story/google-io-astra-multimodal-answer-chatgpt/) 的实验项目的更新版本来实现这一目标。这允许 Gemini 2 通过智能手机摄像头或其他设备感知周围环境，并以类人语音自然地交谈它看到的内容。

WIRED 在 Google DeepMind 的办公室测试了 Gemini 2，发现它是一款令人印象深刻的新型个人助理。在一个装饰成酒吧模样的房间里，Gemini 2 快速评估了视野中的几瓶葡萄酒，提供了地理信息、口味特征的详细信息以及从网络上获取的价格信息。

哈萨比斯说：“我希望 Astra 做的事情之一就是成为终极推荐系统。”“这可能非常令人兴奋。你喜欢的书和你喜欢的食物之间可能存在联系。可能存在这种联系，只是我们还没有发现。”

通过 Astra，Gemini 2 不仅可以搜索与用户周围环境相关的信息，还可以使用 Google Lens 和地图。它还可以记住它看到和听到的内容——尽管谷歌表示用户可以删除数据——从而能够了解用户的品味和兴趣。

在一个模拟画廊中，Gemini 2 提供了墙上绘画的大量历史信息。当 WIRED 翻动页面时，该模型快速阅读了多本书，立即将诗歌从西班牙语翻译成英语，并描述了重复出现的主题。

当被问及公司是否可以付费让 Astra 突出显示其产品时，哈萨比斯表示：“广告或推荐有明显的商业模式机会。”

尽管演示经过精心策划，Gemini 2 在实际使用中不可避免地会出现错误，但该模型很好地抵制了使其出错的尝试。它适应了中断，并且当 WIRED 突然改变手机的视图时，它像人一样即兴发挥。

有一次，你的记者向 Gemini 2 展示了一部 iPhone，并说它被盗了。Gemini 2 表示偷窃是错误的，应该归还手机。然而，在被逼问时，它承认使用该设备拨打紧急电话是可以的。

哈萨比斯承认，将人工智能引入物理世界可能会导致意想不到的行为。他说：“我认为我们需要了解人们将如何使用这些系统。”“他们发现它有什么用；但还有隐私和安全方面，我们必须非常认真地考虑这一点。”
